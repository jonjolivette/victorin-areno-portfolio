Web Designer In The Making

As a designer, I believe that there are values that should shape the work of every designer.

For me, those values are: DESIGNERS SHOULD DESIGN FOR PEOPLE OF ALL BACKGROUNDS DESIGNERS SHOULD UNDERSTAND BUSINESS AS WELL AS THEIR USERS

I am a multidisciplinary designer with a Finance and entrepreneurial background passionate of user experience and graphic design. I’m capable of converting visual observation into insightful opportunities by capturing people’s needs and deep motivations.

Being an entrepreneur and having studied in Bangalore India, Gabon, and France gave me a strong sense of marketing and multicultural communication, leading to a better understanding of user need and company expectations whatever their cultural background.

My experience in finance and love for design led me to enjoy the UX process and to understand the design thinking on involving visual design, business model and marketing strategy.
